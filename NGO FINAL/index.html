Our Work

The following are our main avenues of work, based on which the strategies and programme of the Society are designed.

Student & Education Welfare

Our Foundation runs a one-year programme to facilitate school text books for students from low-income communities for class 10th students. Donations to this programme will help the children's to acquire their education and based on their performance we provide them scholarship for higher studies.

Health & Sanitation

We have played a role in delivering sanitation services to communities in rural areas. Our programs commonly focus on building linkages between technical and social realm. We has helped providing funds for the people in the rural areas and has provided free medicines for them. We focus on Satisfying health and social needs of groups of people like women, elderly or any vulnerable group.

Pension Scheme

The NGO provides Old aged person of above belonging to the rural areas and people with no income are eligible under this Scheme. Beneficiaries under this scheme receive will receive Rs 1000/- monthly.We too give annuity for the widow ladies with no source of wage. Additionally for the old ladies who has no source of salary and no member alive in their family.

Skill Development

we started a program for the uneducated and matriculated young ladies. For which we appointed a master to prepare and teach them from the basics of fitting to planning the dress for occasions . And after the completion of course that was offered for free to these students, for the selected 20 students we provided sewing machiene as a gift who can't afford it.

Composed dinner for destitute individuals

We have been actively helping the underprivileged with the essential food items to survive for the people in rural areas. Our organization have been on-ground, reaching to the remotest parts of to provide food to the needy people. We not only provided food in the rural areas but also focused on providing food for the people who are unemployed.

Ambulance Service

We have one ambulance service with facilities of a stretcher and oxygen, the service will be provided to people in the rural and urban areas which is free of cost. We propose to have more such ambulances at the earliest in the future. We also had provided ambulance service during the peak pandemic for 4 months in the rural areas. Our ambulance service is still going strong and the people are benefitting from it.
